Yogas in Your Birth Chart

Gajakesari Yoga

This yoga forms when Jupiter is in a kendra (1st, 4th, 7th, or 10th) from the Moon. It brings intelligence, respect, and prosperity.

Budhaditya Yoga

Occurs when Mercury and Sun are in the same house. It enhances intellect, analytical skills, and communication.

Raja Yoga

Raja Yoga is formed by the combination of lords of kendras and trikonas. It signifies fame, status, and power.
